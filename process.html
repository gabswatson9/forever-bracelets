How to Make a Friendship Bracelet

Home About Products How It's Made Gallery Feedback

Supplies Needed:

  • Embroidery thread
  • Scissors
  • Tape or clipboard
  • Beads (optional)

Steps:

  1. Cut several strands of thread.
  2. Tie a knot at the top.
  3. Tape it down to a surface.
  4. Start braiding or knotting patterns.
  5. Continue until desired length.
  6. Tie a knot at the end.
