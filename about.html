About Our Business

Forever Bracelets was founded by Rose Lake, who wanted to create meaningful handmade jewelry that feels personal, not just decorative. Each piece is designed to represent the connections that matter most. Friendship and memories: the moments that stay with you long after they happen. Whether it’s a gift for someone you love or something you keep for yourself, every bracelet is made to feel meaningful every time you wear it. Forever Bracelets also helps creative minds create friendship bracelets of their own.

Our goal is to provide high quality, customizable bracelets that symbolize connection and creativity.
